PROJECTS :

Crosscut Hollow :

Learn More >>

Fight your way through a laboratory in the remnants of humanity after they were forced underground. Play as Sam, a failed lab experiment as you combat the inhabitants of what remains. Locked doors and difficult terrain pose a challenge to be overcome, as you manage your health and ammunition through these ruins.

Heximus :

Learn More >>

A casual local multiplayer game to be played with friends. Plant bombs on the field to increase your territory and gain upgrades. Can you take out your opponent before succumbing to one of their bombs?

Apogee Station:

Learn More >>

Defend against ever-increasing waves of enemy ships intent on destroying your station and your only hopes of survival. From the spritely Scout Craft to the behemoth Eclipse Class Carrier, you’ll need to probe their weaknesses and find the right combination of towers to fend off the onslaught.

Pentix :

Learn More >>

Try to build a tower that manages to reach the top of the board in this printable single-player card game. Drawing different cards will dictate how you are able to build, and how the incoming attacks will land. Will you be able to complete your tower before it collapses under its own weight?

Indomitable :

Learn More >>

Become truly indomitable in this printable two player card game. You’ll need to manage units, your board, and your resources to out-maneuver your opponent. With four unique commanders to choose from, can you use their special abilities and capitalize on your opponent’s blunders to take control?

Snowflake Scramble :

Learn More >>

Face off against friends in this friendly local multiplayer game. Collect falling snow, and avoid collecting any of the debris lest you drop what you’ve collected. Will you be able to complete a snowman before your opponent?
