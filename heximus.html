Project Reflection :

This was the first digital game I polished to a presentable state. At this point, much of the development process for games was fairly new to me, and many of the technologies and tools presented me with a challenging learning curve. The creation of UI elements, sound effects, 3-D models, and music, along with the programming of the game gave me a sense for the different roles usually delegated in group projects.
